Information:

Downtown Harrisonburg has many different restaurants, bars, and a very lively culture. In Downtown Harrisonburg, there are approximately thirty restaurants and bars. There are many shops and classic sites that should not be missed when visiting or exploring the area. Downtown Harrisonburg occupies about 40-blocks of street with student housing along the way. Downtown Harrisonburg provides quant houses and apartments for James Madison students. The walking distance from Downtown to JMU is an easy 10-15 minute walk, which is perfect for any student.


Best Restaurants to Visit:

There are many delicious restaurants downtown with a surplus of menu options at each different place. Two of the most popular places to eat are Jack Brown's (top left) and Jimmy Madison's (bottom left). Each of these restaurants are top rated and have a wonderful menu selection. Jack Brown's is known for their delicious, juicy burgers and excellent beers on tap. Jimmy Madison's is also known for their delicious fried pickles and crab dip. Come dine at either of these restaurants and you will not be disapointed!
